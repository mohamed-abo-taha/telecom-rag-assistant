# Billing and Payments

- Bills are issued on the 1st of each month and are due within 14 days.
- Payment methods: credit or debit card, bank transfer, and cash at retail stores.
- Late payment: a 2 USD fee applies after the due date; service is suspended after 30 days of
  non-payment.
- Autopay can be enabled in the app under Billing > Autopay; it charges the saved card on the due date.
- Refunds for overcharges are credited to the next bill within one billing cycle.
- To dispute a charge, open a ticket in the app within 60 days of the bill date.

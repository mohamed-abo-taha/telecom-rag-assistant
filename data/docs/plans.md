# Mobile Plans

## Prepaid
- Starter: 5 USD/month, 5 GB data, 100 national minutes.
- Smart: 15 USD/month, 25 GB data, unlimited national minutes, 100 international minutes.

## Postpaid
- Unlimited Basic: 30 USD/month, unlimited national data (speed capped to 5 Mbps after 100 GB),
  unlimited national calls.
- Unlimited Pro: 50 USD/month, unlimited national data at full speed, unlimited national plus
  300 international minutes, and 10 GB roaming data included.

Plan changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. You can change your plan once per
cycle from the app.
